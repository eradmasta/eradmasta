About me

I am a civil engineer with 13 years of experience both in Poland (10 yrs) and abroad (3 yrs in USA).

At the beginning of 2019, I became interested in front-end development and started learning on my own. Front-end development allows to combine my biggest strengths: analytical thinking and persistence in problem solving with my artistic side, so I decided to change my career path. To level up my skills, I made a decision to take part in the Junior Front-end Developer bootcamp at the infoShare Academy. It gave me a chance to work in teams with other developers, utilizing range of front end technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, ReactJS.

Now, I am searching for my first job as a junior front-end developer in a people-oriented team with a desire to share their knowledge.
